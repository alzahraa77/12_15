Welcome to GratiVibe!

GratiVibe is a website designed to support mental health and mindfulness through a variety of interactive tools. Our platform includes features like a gratitude diary to help users reflect on positive experiences, daily affirmations to foster self-compassion, a mental health guide to give advice and encourage mental clarity, a self-care to-do list for tracking well-being tasks, and a mood tracker to help visualize user feelings weekly.

Vision

A world where mental wellness and gratitude are part of everyday life.

Mission

To empower individuals with tools and resources for mental health and personal growth.
